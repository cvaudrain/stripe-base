Who?

You, Your Team, & The QVR Rotaract Club

You and up to 7 friends will test your trivia knowledge and compete for prizes. Bring your A-game for a friendly competition among the trivia buffs of the Quiet Corner!

Where?

The Putnam Elk's Lodge

Located at 64 Edmond St. Putnam, CT, the Putnam Elk's Lodge is a community-favorite venue that we're thrilled to partner with for this event!

Why?

For Young Scholars in The Quiet Corner!

Each year, our Trivia Tournament proceeds go directly towards funding scholarships for deserving, distinguished, college-bound students who embody the spirit of service in our community.

Your participation allows us to offer these scholarships and invest in the future generation of community leaders!
